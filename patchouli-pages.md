# Overworld Progression
icon "minecraft:grass_block"

Obtaining **Copper Tools** is the first step in a delver's journey.
Stone tools have been removed in favour of Copper, which has become
a fundamental overworld ore, and unlike in vanilla, may be mined with a wooden pickaxe.

Iron can be rarely found embedded within deepslate.
A copper pickaxe is required for the excavation of deeplate and iron ore.

An Iron Pickaxe is required to mine through the bedrock of the overworld,
significant preparation is recommended before entering the layers below.

# Delvers Progression
icon : "minecraft:amethyst_block"

Silver and gold ore are present here, both an upgrade from Iron each with their own benefits.

Gold is higher durability than vanilla and comes with innate effects pre applied such as speed,
fortune II and looting II.

Silver Serves more as a direct upgrade from Iron, although with slightly less durability.

Ore Geodes are abudant here, to obtain diamond look for a Diamond ore geode. There is also
other resources geodes but we'll leave that to you to discover.

A diamond pickaxe is required to mine through into the next layer!

# The Undergarden
icon: "undergarden:deepturf_block"

You will need to obtain Utherium in the undergarden. This can be obtained by either killing various
hostile undergarden mobs, or by finding Utherium Ore.

You will also need to obtain Regalium. You can trade with wandering Stoneborns to obtain it or
find Regalium Ore.

While exploring around obtaining Utherium and Regalium keep an eye out for the Forgotten Catacombs, as 
completing this dungeon is required!

Once you've killed the forgotten guardians of the catacombs you may craft the explorers compass.

You must use the explorers compass to find the Void Blossom.

Delvers hoping to slay the Void Blossom should obtain Full Utherium armor before fighting the Void blossom.

Once the Void Blossom has been slain, craft a forgotten bottle, and bring a Crystal Fruit
To the Stoneborn, and as a reward for slaying the void blossom they will brew you a special Acid used
to Weaken the Hardened roots preventing you from mining below the Undergarden.

To Mine through the Weakened Roots you will need to craft a Cloggrum Pickaxe and A Forgotten ingot and
Trade it with a Stoneborn in order to obtain the all powerful Forgotten Pickaxe.
