# Grappling Hooks
icon: "grapplemod:grapplinghook"

**Grappling Hooks** are an essential tool for potential delvers.
Look out for slimes during your time in the overworld; 
they can be found roaming on any biome on the surface

Hold [SHIFT] while hovering over the hook to see controls.


# Overworld Progression
icon "minecraft:grass_block"

Obtaining **Copper Tools** is the first step in a delver's journey.
Stone tools have been removed in favour of Copper, which has become
a fundamental overworld ore, and unlike in vanilla, may be mined with a wooden pickaxe.

Iron can be rarely found embedded within deepslate.
A copper pickaxe is required for the excavation of deeplate and iron ore.

An Iron Pickaxe is required to mine through the bedrock of the overworld,
significant preparation is recommended before entering the layers below.
